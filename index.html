Core rules

  • Four players in two teams, partners sitting opposite.
  • Trump is chosen after each player sees only four cards.
  • You must follow suit if you can.
  • Five to seven tricks scores 1 token for trump callers, 2 for defenders.
  • All 8 tricks scores 3 tokens, and a 4-4 tie carries a bonus token to the next winning hand.

    Why Omi is special

    Mid-deal trump choice

    Unlike most trick-taking games, you commit to trump with only half your hand revealed.

    Defender pressure

    If defenders win the hand, they score more than the trump-calling side for a normal hand.

    Carry-over tension

    A 4-4 split pushes a bonus token into the next hand, which can swing a close match fast.
